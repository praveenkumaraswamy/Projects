Rules and Regulations

  • Library Rules
  • 1Readers should not mark, underline, write, or tear pages or otherwise damage the library documents
  • 2ReadersReaders are requested to handle all Library property carefully
  • 3Readersto avoid damage to it and also not to disturb other readers/users.
  • 4Smoking, eating, sleeping and talking loudly are strictly prohibited in the Library.
  • 5ReadersNo Library material can be taken out of the Library without permission of the Librarian.
  • Members Rules
  • 1Photocopying service is available for library members at nominal charges
  • 2A separate register for books and for members shall be maintained in each library.
  • 3Books, magazines and journals abusing communities or religions shall not be purchased.
  • 4Non-members can use the library resources in the Library premises
  • 5Documents taken out of the shelves must be left on the table Properly.
